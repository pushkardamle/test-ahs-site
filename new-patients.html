New Patients

Welcome to Allied Health Solutions! Here's what you need to know before your first visit.

Direct Access Certified

We are Direct Access certified, which means you can receive a physical therapy evaluation and treatment without first obtaining a physician referral (depending on your insurance plan).

This allows you to start your recovery sooner and saves you time and potential costs. However, some insurance plans may still require a referral, so we recommend checking with your insurance provider.

What to Expect at Your First Visit

1

Comprehensive Evaluation

Your first visit will be a one-hour evaluation. We'll review your medical history, discuss your symptoms and goals, and perform a thorough physical examination. This comprehensive approach allows us to understand not just the problem area, but how your body works as a whole.

2

Personalized Treatment Plan

Based on your evaluation, we'll develop an individualized treatment plan tailored to your specific condition and goals. We'll explain our findings, answer your questions, and discuss the best approach for your recovery.

3

Begin Treatment

If appropriate, we may begin hands-on treatment during your first visit. We'll also provide you with a home exercise program to complement your in-clinic treatment and accelerate your recovery.

4

Ongoing Care

You'll work with the same therapist throughout your treatment for consistent, personalized care. We'll continuously monitor your progress and adjust your treatment plan as needed to ensure optimal results.

What to Bring

Valid insurance card(s)
Photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
Physician referral/prescription (if required by your insurance)
List of current medications
Any relevant medical records, imaging results (X-rays, MRI, etc.)
Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing appropriate for exercise
Athletic shoes

Before Your Visit

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early for your first appointment to complete any necessary paperwork.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call us at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to offer your appointment time to another patient who may be waiting.

Questions?

If you have any questions before your first visit, don't hesitate to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each appointment?

Your initial evaluation is approximately one hour. Follow-up treatment sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, depending on your treatment plan.

Do I need a referral from my doctor?

As a Direct Access certified clinic, you may not need a referral depending on your insurance plan. However, some insurance companies still require a referral for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider or calling our office.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your condition, severity, and individual response to treatment. After your evaluation, we'll discuss an estimated treatment plan and number of visits.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy movement and access to the area being treated. Athletic shoes are recommended. For certain conditions, you may be asked to wear shorts or a tank top.

Will I see the same therapist every visit?

Yes! To ensure consistency and quality of care, you will work with the same therapist throughout your entire treatment.

Can I schedule same-day appointments?

We make every effort to accommodate urgent requests. Please call us to check availability for same-day appointments.
