Our Philosophy and Mission

Our mission is to provide the best physical therapy services in a relaxed and friendly environment. We provide one-on-one hands on care.

We follow evidence based practice and use an integral approach to treatment.

We are Direct Access certified, which means that a patient can receive a physical therapy evaluation and treatment without first obtaining a physician referral.
