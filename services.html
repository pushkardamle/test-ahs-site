Our Services

Comprehensive physical therapy and rehabilitation services tailored to your needs

What We Treat

At Allied Health Solutions, we provide evidence-based physical therapy services for a wide range of conditions. Our experienced therapists use a personalized approach to help you achieve your recovery goals.

Every patient receives an individualized evaluation and treatment plan. We focus on not just treating symptoms, but identifying and addressing the root cause of your condition.

Our Approach

Comprehensive Evaluation

Hour-long initial evaluation examining not just the problem area but the body as a whole

Individualized Programs

Custom exercise programs tailored to your specific condition and personal goals

One-on-One Care

Consistent treatment with the same therapist throughout your entire course of care

Evidence-Based Practice

Treatment protocols backed by the latest research and clinical evidence

What to Expect

1

Initial Evaluation

Your first visit includes a comprehensive one-hour evaluation where we assess your condition, medical history, and functional limitations. We'll discuss your goals and develop a personalized treatment plan.

2

Treatment Sessions

Each session combines hands-on manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, and education. You'll work with the same therapist throughout your treatment for consistent, high-quality care.

3

Home Exercise Program

We'll provide you with a customized home exercise program to complement your in-clinic treatment and accelerate your recovery.

4

Progress Monitoring

We continuously monitor your progress and adjust your treatment plan as needed to ensure you're making optimal gains toward your goals.
